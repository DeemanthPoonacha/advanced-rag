# Acme Corp Employee Handbook & Policies

Welcome to Acme Corporation! This document outlines our core company policies, benefits, and workplace guidelines.

## 1. Remote Work Policy

At Acme Corp, we embrace a hybrid-first work culture.
* **Core Hours:** All employees are expected to be available for collaboration between **10:00 AM and 3:00 PM EST**, regardless of their physical location.
* **Stipend:** Full-time employees receive a one-time remote office setup stipend of **$500 USD** to purchase ergonomic chairs, monitors, or other home office accessories.
* **Internet Subsidy:** We reimburse up to **$50 USD per month** for high-speed internet expenses. Receipts must be submitted via the expense portal by the 5th of each month.

## 2. Paid Time Off (PTO) & Holidays

We believe in a healthy work-life balance and encourage everyone to take time off to recharge.

### Annual Vacation Leave
* **Standard PTO:** Full-time employees accrue **20 days of paid vacation** per calendar year, accrued monthly at a rate of 1.67 days.
* **Carry-over:** A maximum of **5 unused vacation days** can be carried over to the next calendar year. Any additional unused days expire on December 31st.

### Company Holidays
Acme Corp observes the following standard holidays:
1. New Year's Day (January 1st)
2. Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
3. Independence Day (July 4th)
4. Labor Day (First Monday in September)
5. Thanksgiving Day (Fourth Thursday in November)
6. Day after Thanksgiving
7. Christmas Day (December 25th)

## 3. Travel & Expense Reimbursement

All business-related travel must be pre-approved by your department head.

* **Flights:** Standard economy class must be booked for all domestic flights under 6 hours. Business class is permitted only for international flights exceeding 6 hours.
* **Daily Meal Allowance (Per Diem):** The maximum daily reimbursement for meals during business travel is **$75 USD**.
  * Breakfast: Up to $15 USD
  * Lunch: Up to $20 USD
  * Dinner: Up to $40 USD
* **Lodging:** Hotel stays are capped at **$200 USD per night** in standard locations, and **$350 USD per night** in high-cost cities (e.g., New York, San Francisco, London).

## 4. Health & Wellness Benefits

Acme Corp offers comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance starting on your first day of employment.

* **Health Insurance:** Premium is 80% covered by Acme Corp for individuals, and 70% covered for family plans.
* **Wellness Reimbursement:** We offer a wellness stipend of **$30 USD per month** that can be used for gym memberships, fitness classes, or mental health app subscriptions.
